WebCapture Extension - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Privacy First: WebCapture does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All your content is saved directly to your own Google Sheets in your Google Drive.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. The extension operates entirely locally in your browser and connects directly to your Google account.

What the extension accesses:

  • Selected text: Only text you highlight on webpages (processed locally)
  • Page information: URL and title of pages where you save content
  • Google account: Authentication to access your Google Sheets (via Google's secure OAuth)

2. How We Use Your Information

The extension uses your data solely to:

  • Save your selected text to your own Google Sheets
  • Authenticate with Google on your behalf
  • Create and organize your personal content collection

3. Data Storage and Ownership

You own ALL your data.

  • All content is stored in YOUR Google Drive
  • We have no access to your spreadsheets
  • You can export, delete, or modify your data anytime
  • No vendor lock-in - it's your Google Sheet

4. Google API Usage

This extension uses Google APIs to:

  • Authenticate your Google account (OAuth 2.0)
  • Create and write to Google Sheets in your Drive
  • Access your Drive to manage your content spreadsheet

Google's Privacy Policy: Your interaction with Google services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

  • No data transmission to our servers
  • Google OAuth for secure authentication
  • All data remains in Google's secure infrastructure
  • You can revoke extension access anytime in your Google Account settings

6. Third-Party Services

This extension only integrates with Google Sheets API. We do not use any other third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will update this policy if our practices change. Updates will be posted to the Chrome Web Store listing.

8. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy:

  • Email: privacy@webcapture-extension.com
  • Chrome Web Store: Use the support tab on our extension listing

9. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access all your data (it's in your Google Drive)
  • Delete your data (delete the Google Sheet)
  • Export your data (Google Sheets export features)
  • Revoke extension access (Google Account permissions)
  • Uninstall the extension anytime

Summary: WebCapture is designed with privacy as the foundation. Your content is yours, stored in your Google Drive, with no third-party access or data collection on our part.
